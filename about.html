About us

Two generations. One workshop. Built on trust.

Founded in 1976 by Michalis Vrachimis and re-engineered in 2009 by his son Anninos, our Paralimni workshop has grown into one of Cyprus's leading diagnostic and performance-tuning centres — without losing the family-run feel.

Our story

From a one-man garage in '76 to a 700m² diagnostic centre.

Michalis Vrachimis opened the doors of his Paralimni garage in 1976 with a spanner, a workbench and a reputation he built on doing the job right the first time.

Almost fifty years later, that same reputation — friendly, transparent, technically sharp — runs the workshop today. His son Anninos Vrachimis took over the operation in 2009, fresh from earning two degrees in Automotive Engineering at Teesside University (2010–2015) and time in the workshops of Mercedes-Benz and BMW. He brought with him the latest diagnostic kit, dyno facilities and the engineering training to use them properly.

Today the garage spans more than 700 square metres, runs nine ramps and a dyno, and is the authorized service centre for Honda, Isuzu and Kia in the Ammochostos area — as well as the main BHP UK agent in Cyprus for ECU remapping. Despina Vrachimis welcomes every client at reception. The car you drive in stays your car: documented, photographed, explained, returned cleaner than you left it.
