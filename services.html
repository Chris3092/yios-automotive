Services

Every job, dealer-grade.

From the routine MOT and oil change to ECU remaps, DPF/EGR work and complete engine rebuilds — all under one roof, performed by qualified Automotive Engineers using OEM-spec tooling.

01
Diagnostics & MOT

Multi-brand vehicle diagnostics & MOT testing

Our diagnostic bay is equipped with manufacturer-level scan tools and oscilloscopes — the same kit you'd find at a main dealer. If a warning light comes on and another garage can't explain it, this is where it gets solved.

  • Live data analysis across all major ECUs
  • Oscilloscope fault tracing for electrical & sensor issues
  • Pre-MOT inspection and full MOT testing
  • Documented faults with photos & video sent to your phone
02
ECU Remapping

ECU remapping & chip tuning — BHP UK agent in Cyprus

As the official Cyprus agent for BHP UK, we deliver custom remaps written for your engine, your fuel and your driving — not generic off-the-shelf files. Choose more power, more torque, better economy, or all three.

  • Stage 1 / Stage 2 / Stage 3 remapping
  • Economy maps for fleet & high-mileage drivers
  • Petrol, diesel, hybrid & forced-induction supported
  • Dyno-validated power & torque graphs before/after
  • Tuesdays: 20% off all remapping & tuning
03
DPF

DPF cleaning, regeneration & delete

Diesel particulate filters are expensive to replace and easy to clog. We clean and regenerate them properly — saving you time and money — and where the law and use-case allow, we'll remove them and recalibrate the ECU.

  • Off-vehicle DPF cleaning & flow-testing
  • Forced regeneration via diagnostic tool
  • Full DPF delete with ECU recalibration
  • Advice on driving habits to keep your filter healthy
04
EGR & AdBlue

EGR delete & AdBlue system service

Faulty EGR valves and failed AdBlue systems are two of the most common reasons modern diesels get parked. We fix them properly — including correct ECU calibration so no warning lights come back.

  • EGR valve repair, replacement or delete
  • AdBlue system diagnosis, repair & removal
  • Full ECU recalibration to suppress related fault codes
  • Improved fuel economy & engine longevity after deletion
05
Engine

Engine rebuilds, overhauls & forced-induction builds

From a tired daily-driver that needs a fresh top-end to a fully forged performance build for the track — every rebuild starts with a measurement and ends with a dyno run.

  • Top-end & bottom-end rebuilds
  • Cylinder head reconditioning & valve work
  • Forged-internals builds for turbocharged engines
  • Reference build: Mitsubishi Evo IX, 276 → 700 bhp
06
Performance & Dyno

Performance tuning & in-house dyno

Tuning without a dyno is guesswork. Ours sits in-house, so every map is validated under load with proper before/after data. We have a track record of unlocking serious numbers — safely.

  • In-house dyno for power & torque validation
  • Custom maps from 400 bhp to 700 bhp documented builds
  • Turbo & supercharger installation & calibration
  • Cooling & fuelling system upgrades
07
Engine Care

Forte hydrogen engine clean

Forte's hydrogen combustion-engine cleaning removes carbon deposits from valves, pistons and the intake — restoring lost power, smoothing the idle and clearing up emissions, all without touching the internals.

  • Non-invasive — no dismantling required
  • Restores responsiveness & fuel economy
  • Excellent companion to a major service
  • Recommended every 30,000 km
08
General Service

Routine servicing, repairs & main-dealer alternative

Authorized for Honda, Isuzu and Kia — meaning you keep your warranty intact while getting a friendlier price and a more personal service. We're happy to work on every other make too.

  • Manufacturer-schedule servicing with OEM parts
  • Brakes, suspension, clutches, timing belts & chains
  • Air-conditioning service & recharge
  • Free estimates, courtesy car & collection / delivery
  • Complimentary post-service exterior clean
