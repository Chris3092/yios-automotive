Frequently asked questions

Answers, straight from the engineer.

The questions Cyprus drivers ask us every week — about authorized servicing, remapping, DPF/EGR work and how we run the workshop.

Is Vrachimis & Yios an authorized Honda, Isuzu, Kia and BAIC service centre?

Yes — we're the authorized service centre for all four brands in the Ammochostos (Famagusta) district of Cyprus. Servicing with us keeps your manufacturer warranty intact while you get a friendlier price and a more personal service than a main dealer.

Do you offer a warranty on your work?

Yes — every job comes with our standard 2-year warranty on parts and labour, in writing. Electrical parts are excluded. We stand behind the engineering and we put it on paper.

Where is the workshop and how do I find it?

We're at 44 Filotheis, Industrial Zone, Paralimni 5286 — five minutes off the main road, with plenty of parking. Open in Google Maps →

Do you do ECU remapping, and is it legal in Cyprus?

Yes — we're the main BHP UK agent in Cyprus. Remaps are custom-written for your engine, your fuel and your driving, then validated on our in-house dyno with proper before/after graphs. ECU remapping itself is legal in Cyprus. DPF/EGR deletes have specific legal and emissions considerations and we'll advise on what's appropriate for road or off-road use during your free estimate.

What is "Tuesday Tuners' Day"?

Every Tuesday, Anninos offers 20% off all ECU remapping, EGR delete and power tuning. Free estimates, no obligation. Slots fill up — book ahead via WhatsApp on +357 96 350 642.

Do you collect and deliver cars across Cyprus?

Yes. We regularly collect from Larnaca, Nicosia, Limassol, Ayia Napa and Protaras for diagnostic, remap and rebuild jobs, and we offer courtesy vehicles for longer work. Just ask when you book.

Are estimates really free?

Always — and they're documented in writing before any spanner is turned. We send photos and short videos over WhatsApp so you can see the fault before you decide.

Which makes can you service besides Honda, Isuzu and Kia?

Every make on the island. Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Audi, VW, Range Rover, Jaguar, Ford, Toyota, Nissan, Mitsubishi, Porsche, Aston Martin — petrol, diesel and hybrid. Anninos trained in the workshops of Mercedes-Benz and BMW before earning his Automotive Engineering MEng at Teesside University.

What languages do you speak?

English, Greek and Russian — the whole team is multilingual, including reception.

Need a diagnostic, MOT or a Tuesday remap?

Free estimates, courtesy vehicles, transparent quotes. We're 5 minutes off the main road, in the Paralimni industrial zone.
